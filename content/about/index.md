## Profile

Lead Solution Architect with over 10 years of experience responsible for complex enterprise systems and critical project environments. Stabilizes delivery, establishes governance structures, and transforms unstable systems into scalable, reliable architectures.

Current focus: **integrating AI into existing systems and development processes**. Designs and implements AI-powered solutions — particularly in the areas of RAG, LLM-based systems, and SDLC automation — with the goal of significantly improving developer productivity and reducing time-to-market.

Combines deep architecture expertise (Cloud-Native, Microservices, SOA) with a strong focus on **business impact**. Drives solutions end-to-end — from strategic positioning and architectural decision-making to successful implementation in production environments.

Experienced in leading distributed teams (up to 14 members, including nearshore), mentoring architects, and communicating complex architectural decisions to management and stakeholders.
